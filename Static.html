---Gudeg---

Gudeg is a special food of Yogyakarta and Central Java, Indonesia which is made from young jackfruit cooked with coconut milk. It takes hours to make this dish. The brown color is usually produced by teak leaves which are cooked together. why I like warm so much ?. in addition to its distinctive taste from authentic Indonesian spices, gideng also has a sweet and crunchy taste.

Materials Needed To Make A Delicious And Simple GUDEG

MAIN MATERIALS GUDEG

  • 800 gram young jackfruit
  • 1/2 chicken
  • 2 cm galangal
  • 1,000 ml young coconut water
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1,000 ml coconut milk taken from 1 1/2 coconut
  • 5 teak leaves

Gudeg Seasoning

  • 15 grains of onion
  • 8 grains of hazelnut
  • 135 grams of brown sugar
  • 6 cloves of garlic Salt to taste
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons coriander

How To Prepare GUDEG Ingradients

  1. The first time, please clean the jackfruit that you have prepared. Then cut the jackfruit with a thickness of about 2 cm.
  2. After that, memarkan or geprek galangal.
  3. Clean the chicken that you have prepared. If it has been cut into 4 parts
  4. Blend the spices as mentioned above.
